About Us

About Bethany Hill, Weluisa Farm Bethany Hill, Weluisa Farm is a perfect place for you to relax, enjoy nature and bond with your loved ones. It is a great venue to unwind from all your daily stress. Different events like birthdays, weddings, reunions and other life events can be held in the venue. Catering services are also offered. Entrance fee is P20/person. Food and Drinks are available. Short orders are also available and you can request for any food that you wish to be served, 2-3 days before your visit. Bringing your own food is allowed without any corkage fee but table/s must be rented for P50. SMOKING and DRINKING of LIQUOR is strictly prohibited any Violators will be BANNED. We suggest that you visit early morning (6-7am) to see the Sunrise or late afternoon (4-6pm) to see the Sunset. Bring your caps, jackets and umbrellas since its cold most of the time because of the cold mountain breeze.
